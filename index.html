Summary

The facts and figures show that the members of the Kellogg Full-Time MBA class of 2014 were in high demand from recruiters across a broad range of industries and graduates. The diversity of companies, their geographies and the positions filled attests to the broad appeal of the Kellogg graduate in virtually every sector of the global economy.
